CASUAL ME

[Slide 1/3]


I once pursued in astrophysics. Mostly because of all the formulas and my favorite physicist - Stephen Hawking. Pursued until the point of wanting to be an astrophysicist. Yet here I am making and completing my own personal website, because of some personal reason, or reasons. To others, astrophysics is a rare career or even unusual to take as a permenant job. People said: "Nah, you can't never get a job.", "No, the future needs IT-based employees.", "You need to think between money and interest.", and so on. The first day I enrolled in a Software Engineering diploma course, I promised myself, not just any angry or sad promise, but a neutral and strong promise, that I must and I will use my skills and knowledge in Software Engineering to get a job, either well-paid or under-paid, as long as there is a monthly salary, I will use my own savings for my longed astrophysics course. This is the path I paved for myself since that very day and nothing is going to destroy it, even if I am 40-50 years old. Why settle down when you have more to pursue and achieve?

[Slide 2/3]


To be frank, or as you can see, I love objects that exist in space. Silent, dangerous, yet heavenly. I think of astronomy is the field where I can find my life purpose. I don't have a mind of businessman, nor a skill of marketing, nor a voice of influencer. You might tell me that I can learn or study those skills, but it is where interest wins. One can't fold a twig like paper, because twig has "twig" properties while paper has its. I am curious and want to know the answer of every unsolved questions or problems of space or even time. Sounds ridiculous? It may be but there are more ridiculous things going on when you look into the dark abyss of space. I will keep thinking and pondering until my last day that I stop asking why. That's me, the true me.

[Slide 3/3]


Astrophysics, checked. Astronomy, checked. Astrology, yes! Also checked. Astrology is just a little weird past-time activity for me when I was in secondary school. I used to print out all relevant information about the horoscopes and be an astrologist in my class when there is no teacher around. Why? Because it is fun to start a chat with friends in a weird manner. Not to be superstitious, but there is a game I was and am still playing, I drew my first character who is an astrologist. And I was a 10% astrologist once. You get what I mean. Uh... Anyways, astrology is just travel guide in life like all religions do while we are the one who controls and decides the direction of the steering wheel.
